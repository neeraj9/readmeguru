Once daksh prajapati organised a yagna at his place and invited other gods and their spouses but did not extend invitation to lord Mahesh and Sati although the letter happened to be his son-in-law and daughter respectively. Sati however got the news of this event from others. she prayed the lord Shiva to permit her to attend the event but the lord refused by saying "one should never visit anyone uninvited". Despite that Sati visited her father's place and she was not welcomed by anyone in the family. Enraged by this attitude she jumped in the fireplace (hawan kundi) and lost her life.

The sad news came to lord Shiva. He also became very angry and created veerbhadra who went to the place of daksh prajapati and killed him.

What lesson we get from this is that we should never visit any place uninvited. This is available in chapters 1-5 of skanda 4 of Shrimad bhagvata mahapuranam.

In the present scenario we should make it a point that we don't visit any place uninvited and more so become very choosy in visiting any place even with extensive warm invitation to prevent the spread of Covid-19. This is more true in the case of children who are innocent but they should be prevented and should be advised to follow this dictum.
