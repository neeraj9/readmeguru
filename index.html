Sanatandharma is hundereds and thousands of years old. It teaches us apart from many other things the "way of life" via near infinite discourses.

Way of Life / Mind and Body

There are near infinite discourses in sanatandharma which teach us the way of live. There are hundereds and thousands of Hindu deities which play a critical role in various holy text discourses. We can understand the true meaning of life by understanding those teachings in deeper form and sometime in isolation of the other details. There are numerous texts / online media for reading the complete story, but none to deep dive into specific learnings which is important to understand the hidden wisdom.
